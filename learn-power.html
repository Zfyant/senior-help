Device Frozen

Recover gracefully from unresponsive screens and stalled apps.

Step 1: Wait and Close

Give the app a minute; try closing via task manager or force quit.

Step 2: Force Restart

Use the correct button combo for your device model to reboot.

Step 3: Free Space

Clear storage and memory; remove unused apps or files.

Step 4: Update System

Apply OS updates and check for app patches addressing freezes.
